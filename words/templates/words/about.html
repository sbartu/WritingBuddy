Wordle is a small project created by Bartu Sivri.

Wordle is a search engine to help writers find and choose different words for their essays, poems, lyrics and more. Type in the word you would like learn the definition and usage of, find a replacement for or find rhyming words for.

It uses Django, Bootstrap 4.3 and Vue.js.
